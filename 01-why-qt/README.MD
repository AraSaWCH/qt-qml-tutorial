![Episode Image](episode_image.png)

## Episode Purpose
In this episode, we'll discuss important factors to consider when deciding to use the Qt framework for your project. Making the right technology choice is crucial for the success of your application, and considering these factors will help you make an informed decision.

We will determine if Qt is right choice, checking following key factors:
1. Purpose
2. Target Platforms
3. UX/UI Requirements
4. Skills and Expertise
5. Industry-specific Considerations
6. Business Factors
